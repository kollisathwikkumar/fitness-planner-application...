MONDAY DIET MENU


Breakfast: Masala oats or poha (flattened rice) with a cup of chai (tea).

Lunch: Roti (whole wheat flatbread) with dal (lentil curry) and a side of mixed vegetable sabzi.

Snack: A serving of fresh fruit chaat with a sprinkle of chaat masala.

Dinner: Tandoori chicken or paneer (grilled chicken or cottage cheese) with brown rice and a cucumber raita (yogurt with cucumber and spices).

Dessert: A small serving of fruit custard or a piece of jaggery (gur) for a sweet ending.
